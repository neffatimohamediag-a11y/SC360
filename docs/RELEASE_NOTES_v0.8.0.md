# SC360 Expedite Intelligence v0.8.0

## Recovery Intelligence and Decision Center

- Added ranked recovery options for every constraint.
- Added inbound air freight, outbound air freight, alternative material, supplier weekend production, internal overtime, partial shipment, and inter-plant inventory transfer proposals.
- Added cost, lead time, coverage, success probability, approval owner, customer impact, and transparent recovery scoring.
- Added a management-ready Decision Proposal with production value protected and value-to-cost ratio.
- Added Decision Center with Pending Approval, Approved, Rejected, Executing, and Completed states.
- Added Approve, Reject, Request Another Option, and Start Execution actions.
- Added browser-local persistence for decisions.
- Updated application branding to v0.8.0.

Product principle: Do not escalate problems. Escalate decisions.
