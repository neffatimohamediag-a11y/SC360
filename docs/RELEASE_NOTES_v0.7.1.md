# SC360 Expedite Intelligence v0.7.1

## Activated workflows
- Create Recovery Mission opens a prefilled mission form.
- Missions persist in browser local storage.
- Mission Center supports assignment, due dates, notes and status progression.
- Statuses: Open, In Progress, Waiting Supplier, Recovered and Closed.
- Missions can be exported to CSV or deleted.
- Sidebar navigation now opens Planner, Supplier, Cell, Snapshot and Mission workspaces.
- Supplier and production-cell panel arrows are active.
